Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Butter
  • Tomato Hybrid
  • Chilli flakes
  • Oregano
  • Onion
  • garlic
  • Cheese slice
  • Fresh Cream
  • Veggies

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta:

    Cook 2 cups pasta in 4-6 cups salted boiling water until al dente (8-10 mins).

    Reserve ½-¾ cup pasta water, drain, rinse cold, and set aside.

  2. Sauté base:

    Heat 1-2 tbsp oil/butter in pan.

    Add 4-6 chopped garlic cloves; sauté 1 min. Add ½ chopped onion (optional); cook until golden.

  3. Cook sauce:

    Stir in 1 cup tomato puree (blend 4-6 tomatoes) or paste.

    Simmer 5-7 mins until raw smell gone. Add chili flakes, oregano, pepper, salt, 1 tsp sugar.

  4. Combine:

    Pour reserved pasta water into sauce; boil 1-2 mins.

    Toss in drained pasta until coated evenly

  5. Serve:

    Garnish with grated cheese, extra pepper.

    Enjoy hot post-gym or as quick meal.
