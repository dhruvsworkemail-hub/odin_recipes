Cheese Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Bread Slice
  • 2 Cheese Slice
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • Chilli flakes
  • Onion
  • Tomato

Steps:

  1. Prep: spread butter on outer sides of both bread slices. Place cheese (and optional fillings) between unbuttered sides.
  2. Cook: Heat pan on medium-low. Add sandwich; cook 1-2 mins per side until golden and cheese melts, pressing gently.
  3. Serve: Slice diagonally, enjoy hot with ketchup or tea.
