Omelette

Description

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Green onion
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Prep eggs: Whisk 2-3 eggs in a bowl with salt, pepper, and optional chopped onion, tomato, chili, or herbs. Add 1 tsp water for fluffiness.
  2. Cook base:Heat 1 tsp butter/oil in nonstick pan over medium-low. Pour eggs, tilt to spread evenly. Cook 1-2 mins until edges set; gently push cooked edges to center so raw egg flows underneath.
  3. Fill & fold:Add cheese/veggies on one side when mostly set. Fold in half, slide onto plate. Serve hot.
